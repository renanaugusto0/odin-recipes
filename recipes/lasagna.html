Lasagna

The pasta layers are stacked, creating a delightful casserole.

Principals Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Ragù
  • Béchamel Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Seasonings

Principal Way To Do Lasagna

  1. Prepare the Pasta: Boil wide, flat lasagna sheets until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Ragù: In a skillet, brown ground beef or pork with onions and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, herbs (like basil and oregano), salt, and pepper. Simmer until thickened.
  3. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce: In a separate saucepan, melt butter, add flour, and cook until golden. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  4. Layer the Lasagna: In a baking dish, start with a layer of ragù, followed by pasta sheets, béchamel, and a mix of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat until all ingredients are used up.
  5. Bake and Serve: Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest before serving.
