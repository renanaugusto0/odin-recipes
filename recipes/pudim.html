Pudim

Principals Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cans of whole milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (opcional)

Principal Way To Do Pudim

  1. Make the Caramel: Melt the sugar in a pan over medium heat until golden. Pour into a bundt pan, coating the bottom and sides. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Pudim:Blend the condensed milk, whole milk, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth. Pour into the caramel-coated pan.
  3. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil. Place the pan in a larger baking dish filled with hot water (water bath). Bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until set.
  4. Cool and Unmold: Let it cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours. To unmold, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate.
