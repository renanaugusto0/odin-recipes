Brigadeiro

Principals Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for coating)

Principal Way To Do Brigadeiro

  1. In a saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 10 minutes)
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. Grease your hands with butter, then shape the mixture into small balls.
  5. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles to coat them.
  6. Place the brigadeiros in mini paper cups and serve.
